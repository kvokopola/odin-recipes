Almond Baklava

Description:

This almond baklava is delicious, especially if you love almonds. I have been asked by several of my Turkish friends to share the recipe and I have been told that cinnamon adds a nice touch.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1/4 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 pounds chopped almonds
  • 2 tablespoons whtie sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 1/2 cups unsalted butter, melted
  • 1(16 ounce) package frozen phyllo pastry, thawed
